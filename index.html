About me

Hello, I am Rochelle Cohn! I attended the School of Interactive Games and Media at the Rochester Institute of Technology, where I graduated as part of the class of 2018 with Summa Cum Laude. I am currently an Interactive Simulation Programmer. Previously, I was an associate software-engineer at American Greetings. I worked on Blue Mountain animated ecards, game ecards, and various Jacquie Lawson Unity applications. I was the president of the Arts and Culture society — a workplace club that collaborated with other groups and hosted various events that included charity raffles and chili cook-offs. In the past, I've volunteered as the events director for a local nerd-culture convention for two years, and I have volunteered in various staff positio ns for three. As the events director, I would create the weekend convention schedule and collaborate with various high profile talent to accommodate their needs. In my spare time I like to work on my tabletop game, make Beat Saber maps and volunteer at conventions.
